# 🎵 EDM Kenyan Covers Content & Release Guide

## 🎼 My Music Style

I create modern electronic instrumental covers and remixes of Kenyan music, including:

* 🎸 Kamba Benga instrumental covers
* 🎛️ EDM remixes of traditional Kenyan songs
* 🎤 Instrumental covers of Kenyan urban classics (throwbacks)
* ⛪ EDM versions of Zilizopendwa church songs
* 🎹 Other Kenyan genres (not limited to Kamba)

**Creative philosophy:** *"I tap into the EDM spirit"* by blending Kenyan musical heritage with modern electronic production.
